# Made Thought Design System

**Made Thought** is an independent London branding studio. We build brands for sectors where desire drives the purchase decision — Beauty, Places, Taste, Lifestyle, Technology, Culture. Our work sits at the intersection of cultural intelligence, strategic rigour, and craft.

This repository is the Made Thought design system for use with Claude Design. It encodes our visual language, typographic hierarchy, component library, and slide presentation system — giving Claude the foundation to produce on-brand work without manual style correction.

## The Four Conditions of Desire

Every brand we work on is measured against these four conditions:

1. **Atmosphere** — What does this brand feel like before you think about it?
2. **Tension** — What productive contradiction makes this brand alive?
3. **Codes** — What signatures make this brand unmistakable across its ecosystem?
4. **Distinctiveness** — Can you identify this brand from a single element, without the logo?

## Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weight | Size range |
|------|----------|--------|------------|
| Display / Hero | Items MT Light (Colophon) | Light (300) | 46px – 180px |
| Page title / Eyebrow | Futura PT Book | Book (400) | 22px, uppercase, tracked |
| Body | Futura PT Book | Book (400) | 28px |
| Footer / Caption | Futura PT Book | Book (400) | 12px |

**Rule:** Items MT Light owns the emotional register. Futura PT Book owns the functional register. They never compete — they alternate.

## Slide format

- **Canvas:** 1920 × 1080px
- **Padding:** 60px all sides
- **Content width:** 1800px
- **Major gap (between sections):** 80px
- **Minor gap (between elements):** 24px
- **Themes:** Light (white bg, black ink) · Dark (black bg, white ink)
- Dark slides are used for covers, section dividers, and end slides only

## Design principles

1. **Restraint is confidence.** MT designs use black, white, and space. No decorative elements, no gradients, no unnecessary colour.
2. **Typography as image.** The Items MT Light display type IS the hero visual. Trust it.
3. **Modularity over custom.** All slides are assembled from a fixed set of components. No one-offs.
4. **Eyebrow + Display + Body.** The three-layer text hierarchy. Eyebrow labels the section (uppercase Futura), Display names the idea (Items MT), Body provides evidence (Futura Book).
5. **Footer always present.** Every slide carries "©2026 Made Thought" + "Private & Confidential" at 12pt Futura, left and right aligned, 14px height.
6. **Dark = moments.** Dark slides are punctuation — covers, dividers, endings. Light slides are content.
7. **Space is not empty.** Generous padding is a design decision, not an oversight.
